Stilliguamish River

The Stillaguamish River is mainly composed of two forks, the longer North Fork Stillaguamish (45 miles (72 km)) and the South Fork Stillaguamish. The two forks join near Arlington. From there the Stillaguamish River proper flows for 22 miles (35 km) to Puget Sound. The river's watershed drains part of the Cascade Range north of Seattle.

Arlington Municipal Airport

Arlington Municipal Airport is a public airport located three miles southwest of the central business district of Arlington. It is owned and operated by the City of Arlington.

Gleneagle Golf Course

The Gleneagle Golf Course was developed and opened in 1993 in Arlington, Washington and is a great experience for any golfer of any skill level.

Olympic Avenue

Olympic Avenue is at the heart of downtown Arlington. Along Olympic Avenue, there are various shops and it is a great location for venues and festivals.

Centennial Trail

The Snohomish County Centennial Trail is a 30-mile-long rail trail in Snohomish County, Washington, connecting the cities of Snohomish, Lake Stevens, and Arlington to Skagit County along the corridor of Washington State Route 9.

Haller Park Bridge

This bridge is not only part of the Centennial Trail but it is also part of Haller Park. Haller Park has beach access, a splash pad picnic areas.
